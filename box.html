Home

Welcome to Rayson Rescue Dogs Shelter, a non-profit organization dedicated to rescuing and rehoming dogs in need in Uganda.

We are Rayson Rescue Dogs Shelter, a registered animal rescue located in Kampala, Uganda.
We are passionate about saving and protecting animals
in Uganda and our vision is to see a world full of happy animals. Since 2018, we have been working hard to save animals in our country and we have managed to save thousands
of animal lives plus the dogs at our shelter and we are only glad that you, our donors and friends, have done the most for us to keep on saving more
and more as much as we may like. We are a team aimed at seeing the mission, vision and goals attained with love from the pawsitive community.
